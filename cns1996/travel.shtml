CNS*1996 Travel, food and lodging

The CNS*96 Meeting site is the Cambridge Center Marriott Hotel in Kendall Square, Cambridge, Massachusetts. For those of you who may not know, Cambridge is right across the Charles River from Boston, and all flight arrangements should be made for Boston's Logan Airport. The hotel is directly adjacent to the Kendall Square subway stop on the Red Line, and is across the street from MIT. From the airport, the hotel can be reached by taxi (about 20 dollars -- 20 minutes with light traffic).

The hotel can also be reached by taking the subway. In Boston the subway is known as the "T", and currently (4/96) costs $0.85 for most rides. At the airport, take a shuttle bus to the subway station, then take the Blue Line "inbound" to Government Center. Change at Government Center to the Green Line inbound. Take the Green Line (any train) to Park Street and change to the Red Line. Take the Red Line outbound to Kendall Square. The whole trip should take about 45 minutes. Subway system maps are sometimes available at the information booth at the Park Street station.

Details are as follows:

Cambridge Center Marriott, Two Cambridge Center, Corner of Broadway and Third Street, Cambridge, Massachusetts, 02142. Reservations can be made at 1-800-228-9290 or (617) 494-6600.

Students will be asked to verify their status on check in with a student ID or other documentation.

Student Housing

For student registrants, 14 single occupancy rooms are available on the Harvard University campus at a total of $148 for all FOUR nights. Due to the limited number of rooms, do NOT assume you have received housing until you are notified that you have.

You must agree to arrive during a brief check-in period arranged with Harvard (July 13, 6 - 8 p.m.) and check out on July 17 (6 -8 p.m). We cannot guarantee access to keys if you miss the check-in. The rooms have single beds and private bathrooms, but do not have telephones. Rooms are on the undergraduate Harvard campus in Cambridge (a 15 minute subway ride from the conference location at Kendall Square). To register for these rooms e-mail your request to Judy Macias. Please note that the housing requests will be on a first-come-first-served basis. (We will not accept smaller payments for shorter stays, since the conference must pay for the full time period for each room).

Day Care

Day care will be available at the conference for those who inform us in advance of their day care needs. Please send e-mail with

  • name of parent(s),
  • e-mail address,
  • name of children requiring day care,
  • age of children, and
  • estimated times during which children will need day care.

Day care will be available from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Sunday to Wednesday. Note that day care will not be provided during the evening.
