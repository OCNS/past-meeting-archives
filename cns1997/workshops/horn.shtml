July 6 - 10, 1997, Big Sky, Montana

CNS*1997 Workshops

Spiking Neuron and Fast Computation.

David Horn

Some of the topics that may be discussed:

  • Evidence for sparse spiking.
  • Temporal encoding of analog inputs.
  • Mechanisms for rapid computation in systems of spiking neurons.
  • Computational power of neural networks of spiking neurons.
  • Emulation of associative memories in networks of spiking neurons.
  • Neuronal decoding of temporal information.

Speakers:

  • David Horn, Tel Aviv University
  • Simon Thorpe, CNRS, France
  • Thomas Natschlaeger, Technical University, Graz, Austria
