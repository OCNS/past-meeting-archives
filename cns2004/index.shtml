CNS*2004

The Annual Computational Neuroscience Meeting

July 18-20 2004, Baltimore, MD, USA

Table of contents of CNS04 Proceedings Book (pdf)

The annual Computational Neuroscience Meeting will be held at the historic Radisson Plaza Lord hotel in Baltimore, MD, USA July 18th - 20th, 2004. The main meeting will be followed by two days of workshops on July 21st and 22nd. In conjunction, the "2004 Annual Symposium, University of Maryland Program in Neuroscience: Computation in the Olfactory System" will be held as a satellite symposium to CNS*04 on Saturday, July 17th.
