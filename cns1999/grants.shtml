CNS*1999 Travel Grant Information

In order to process your travel grant we need a copy of your passport.

Also, foreign nationals attending the CNS*99 conference need to enter the U.S. on a Business Visa in order to be eligible for reimbursement for travel expenses. This means that you will need to go to the American Consulate in your respective country and request a B-1 Business Visa. Upon entering the country, you will receive a little white card called "I-94" valid for up to 6 months. Please make sure you photocopy the I-94 card (as well as the passport). We will need it in order to process your paperwork.

People from the following countries don't need to apply for the Business Visa. Instead, you can just enter the U.S. on the "Visa Waiver for Business". All you need to do is come to the country and, upon entry, you will be issued a green I-94 card with the initials "WB" written in and you will be issued a green I-94 card with the initials "WB" written in and valid for up to 3 months. Again, make sure to photocopy this card before you come to CNS*99.
