CNS*1999

The Annual Computational Neuroscience Meeting

July 18-22, 1999, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

CNS*1999 Directions to the Hilton Pittsburgh and towers

600 COMMONWEALTH PLACE
(412) 391-4600

FROM THE EAST :

PA Turnpike (1-76 West) to Exit #6 Monroeville-Pittsburgh onto Parkway 376 West. Exit 376 at the #2 Exit which is Stanwix Street (left-hand exit). At the light, make a left onto Fort Pitt Boulevard. Take the first right onto Commonweath Place. The Hilton is on the right.

FROM THE WEST :

PA Turnpike (I-76 East) to Exit #3 onto Route 19 South which will go into 1-79 South. 1-79 merges into 1-279. Follow 279 South signs for the Airport/Fort Pitt Bridge. On the bridge, get into the second lane from the left (it will say Ft. Duquesne Blvd.). Exit left off the bridge and go down the ramp to Fort Duquesne Boulevard. At the first street light, make a right onto Stanwix Street. At the next light, make a right onto Liberty Avenue. Go to the next traffic light and make a right. This is Commonwealth Place, and the Hilton is directly on your right.

FROM THE NORTH :

1-79 South merges into 279 South. Follow 279 South signs for the Airport/Fort Pitt Bridge. Follow this sign over the Fort Duquesne Bridge and take the Fort Duquesne Boulevard ramp. At the first light, turn right onto Stanwix Street. At the next light, make a right onto Liberty Avenue. At the next light, make a right onto Commonwealth Place. The Hilton is on your right.

FROM PITTSBURGH INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT :

When you exit the airport, take a left and follow the signs to Pittsburgh to the Fort Pitt Tunnel. Go through the tunnel which will bring you out onto the Fort Pitt Bridge. Come off the bridge and at the first light, make a left onto Commonwealth Place. The Hilton is on your right.

FROM THE SOUTH :

Take 279 North through the Fort Pitt Tunnel and over the Fort Pitt Brige to Exit #9 (Liberty Avenue). At the first light, make a left onto Commmonwealth Place. The Hilton is on your right.

FROM OAKLAND :

Follow Fifth Avenue and stay in the second lane from the left. Go through approximately 8 traffic lights and make a left onto Craft Avenue. At the first light, make a right onto Forbes Avenue. Follow signs for Downtown. The road will yield onto 376 West. Take Exit #2 (Stanwix Street) which will be a left-hand exit. At the first light, make a left onto Fort Pitt Boulevard. Make the first right onto Commonwealth Place. The Hilton is on your right.
